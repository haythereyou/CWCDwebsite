Steps to Order Water from the CWCD website

By registering with CWCD, you can create, manage, and delete water orders from home. You can also check previous orders or your account balance. Remember, you can always drop off a card in person at the CWCD office with or without registering.

Step 1: Create an account

  1. Go to the CWCD Account Sign Up page.
  2. Enter your email address.
      3. Tip: Your email will serve as your username.
  3. Enter your password.
      4. Tip: You password length must be at least characters with at least one capital letter and one number.
  4. Click Register.

Step 2: Complete the CWCD Online Acknowledgment Form

  1. Download, complete, and print the CWCD online acknowledgment form.
  2. On the form, you can specify one or more water accounts associated with your properties.
  3. You can also specify other individuals that can make water orders on your behalf.

Step 3: Come to the CWCD office with your completed form

  1. Please come during office hours
  2. Office hours are Monday and Thursday, 3-5 PM
